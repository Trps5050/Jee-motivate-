Top Tips to Crack JEE

Smart Study. Strong Concepts. Full Dedication.

1. Smart Planning

Topic ko divide karo – easy, moderate, tough. Pehle easy aur moderate topics finish karo taaki motivation bana rahe.

2. NCERT is your base

Especially for Chemistry, NCERT line-by-line yaad karo. Direct questions aate hain.

3. PYQs = JEE ka Mirror

Previous year questions solve karo har chapter ke baad. Pattern aur concept dono samajh aayega.

4. Revision Strategy

Har Sunday ya 15 din me ek revision ka time fix rakho. Jo bhool gaye ho, wo phir se yaad ho jaata hai.

5. Test Yourself

Mock tests do har week – uske analysis karo. Galtiyon pe kaam karo, accuracy improve karo.

6. Consistency > Perfection

Roz thoda thoda padho – even 4–5 hours daily agar properly padho to kaafi hota hai.

"JEE crack karne ke liye topper banna zaroori nahi, bas regular aur sincere rehna zaroori hai."
