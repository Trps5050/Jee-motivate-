Kaise Manage Karein School Aur JEE ki Taiyari Saath-Saath

JEE ki preparation khud mein hi ek bada challenge hota hai, lekin jab aapko school aur boards ke saath balance karna padta hai, toh stress double ho jata hai. Lekin smart planning aur focus se sab kuch possible hai!

1. Time Table is Must

Har successful student ka secret hota hai ek solid time table. School ke baad har din kam se kam 4-5 ghante JEE ke liye nikaalein. Boards aur JEE ke syllabus mein 60-70% topics common hote hain — use wisely!

2. One Target – Multiple Benefits

Jab aap Physics, Chemistry, ya Math padh rahe ho toh try karo ki dono board aur JEE ke point of view se samjho. Jaise board ke liye theory important hai, toh JEE ke liye usi topic ke numericals practice karo.

3. Weekends are Gold

Weekend mein zyada waqt hota hai. Is samay mock tests do, weak topics revise karo aur school homework bhi finish karo.

4. Notes Banao Smartly

Jo notes banate ho, unhe aise likho ki board exams ke liye bhi use ho sake aur JEE ke liye bhi. Color codes, short tricks, aur formula sheets bana lo.

5. Take Breaks – Mental Health Zaruri Hai

Kewal padhai se burnout ho sakta hai. Har 45-50 minutes padhai ke baad chhota break lo. Meditation ya thoda walk bhi help karega.

6. Doubts Mat Chhodo

Kahin bhi atakte ho toh turant pucho — teachers se, YouTube pe search karo ya online community ka use karo.
